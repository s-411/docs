# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 5, 2026**

HeadsUp ("we", "our", or "us") operates the HeadsUp - Subscription Tracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. We do not store passwords in plain text.

### Subscription Data
The App allows you to manually enter and track your subscriptions. This data is stored securely and associated with your account.

### Device Information
We may collect device identifiers and push notification tokens solely for the purpose of delivering renewal reminder notifications.

### Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the App, such as which features are used most frequently. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the subscription tracking service
- Send you renewal reminder notifications (with your permission)
- Improve and optimize the App
- Respond to support requests

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard encryption. We use Convex as our backend provider, which employs robust security measures including encryption at rest and in transit.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with service providers essential to operating the App (e.g., our cloud infrastructure provider).

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data at any time through the App
- **Correct** any inaccurate information in your account
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your subscription data

To exercise any of these rights, use the relevant features in the App or contact us at support@headsupapp.com.

## Push Notifications

We request permission to send push notifications solely to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals. You can disable notifications at any time through the App settings or your device settings.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App.
